Lago di Braies

Lago di Braies is magical. It looks just like a fairy tale landscape belonging to the elves and faeries. Its pristine turquoise waters provide the most stunning glass-like reflection of the Croda Del Becco, and its shores are framed with perfect emerald-green pine trees and snow-capped mountains. It is just breathtaking.
